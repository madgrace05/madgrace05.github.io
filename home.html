Party Preparation Calender

Use this schedule to be on top of party planning


    Do a month before party:

  • Make decorations
  • Make invitations
  • Reserve party location



    • Do a week before party:

  • Make sure everyone has RSVP-ed
  • Buy more decorations (if need)
  • Make mini cake bases (freeze them after they cool and defrost them on the day of the party)
  • Pack everything (except food) for party



    • Do a day before party:

  • Prepare sides
  • Prepare main dish
  • Prepare small desserts
  • Make icing for cake decorating
  • Put icing into pipping bags
  • Defrost cakes
  • Double check that everything has been packed
