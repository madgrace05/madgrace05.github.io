A step-by-step guild to making invitations!

This guide will follow in this order...

  1. Make prints and cut-outs
  2. Gather materials
  3. Assemble invites
  4. Package and give out the invites


Step 1: Make print and cut-outs

I made mine by using Google Slides and a free website called RemoveBG (it removes backgrounds).

Make sure that you have the following items for printing:
  1. invitation pages (enough for all the people your inviting and add one extra)
  2. images of things like flowers, bows, and buttons that you can cut and glue to add decoration to the invite
  3. name labels or anything you would like to add to the envelope

Make sure to include this information on the invite pages

  • Where the party is going to be
  • Where to park cars
  • What time the party will start and end
  • What day the party will be on
  • How to contact you with questions
  • How to RSVP (I told them to text me and gave my number)
  • What to wear (I asked my guests to wear an all white outfit and I wore a colorful dress)
  • and other things like what to bring and what food will be there

    Step 2: Gather materials

    This step is definitely the most important one! No guests = no party!

    What you need to collect:

    • scissors
    • hot glue and glue stick
    • colorful cardstock paper
    • invite print-out
    • collage print-outs

    Step 3: Assemble invites

    Make sure you give yourself enough time to thoughtfully put the invites together! I suggest putting on your fave show!

    Follow these steps to make cute invites:

    1. make sure you have enough of your materials and have cut out all of your print outs.
    2. start by glueing the invite pages to the colorful cardstock paper.
    3. add any print out images to the invites to give them a little creativity.
    4. make the envelops by adding the guest's name and some decorations. Don't forget to add the address and return address if you plan on actually mailing the invites.
