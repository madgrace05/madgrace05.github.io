Snacks

Snack Ideas

I put lots of fruit, veggies, and humus out for my friends to enjoy! Here are other ideas for your own party!

  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Apples
  • Grapes
  • Strawberries
  • Humus
  • Various cheeses (make sure they will be okay in the hot sun)
  • Various crackers

This website gives a great insite into traditional French picnics!

Main Dish

Sandwich Ideas

For my party, I made turkey and cheese sandwiches and sunbutter and raspberry jam sandwiches (I used sunbutter to have a vegetarian option that my nut-allergy friends could also enjoy)!

Here are other ideas for your own party!

  • Turkey and cheese
  • Sunbutter (sunflower seed butter) and raspberry jam
  • Mozzarella, heirloom tomato, and basil
  • chickpea salad

This website is a great source for ideas and diverse recipes!

Desserts

Sweets Ideas

To celebrate my birthday, one of my activities was for my friends to decorate their own mini cake so I put petit sweets out for them to munch on! Here are other ideas for your own party!

  • Madeleines (my name is Madeline so these were so cute!)
  • Tea cakes
  • Bite sized sponge cake
  • Eclaires
  • Macaroons

This website has such cute and perfect tiny desserts!
