Decorating Mini Cakes

Collect and prepare these items for your cake decorating bar

  • 2x the cake layer to guests in two different flavors (ex: 5 guests = 10 cake layers, 5 choc & 5 van)
  • 3 different flavors of icing (lemon, choc, and strawberry)
  • 3 different fillings (fudge sause, raspberry jam, lemon curd)
  • a bunch of toppings (sprinkles, edible glitter, dried orange slices, dried strawberries)
  • piping bags and spatulas for the icing
  • a cake box for every guest

How to decorate your mini cakes...

Invite your guests to decorate their own personal mini cake! They're sure to love it! Encourage your guests to follow these step by step instructions.

  1. pick 2 cake layers (chocolate or vanilla)
  2. pick a filling
  3. choose an icing
  4. decorate till you drop!

Bouquet Making

Gather all of these materials to set up a perfect flower station

  • 9x flower types
  • 9x flower vases
  • Scissors
  • butcher's paper for each guest
  • Twine
  • Tape

How to get the flower station prepared...

  1. take the flowers out of their packaging. Keep the flower food packets
  2. deleaf the flower stems and take of any buds
  3. cut each stem at a 45° angle so at to let the flowers absore more water
  4. fluff the flower petals and make sure there are no bugs
  5. cut the butcher's paper into large squares to be used for wrapping the bouquets
  6. cut the twine for holding the bouquet together
